Strawberry Jelly

A sweet, perfect go-to recipe for jelly you can put on snacks and eat all you want!

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Sugar

How to make Strawberry Jelly

  1. Wash the strawberries well and cut them in small pieces
  2. Put the strawberries on a wide pan, as well as the sugar, and heat it up
  3. Don't let it burn, keep stirring until it's consistent and doesn't roam around as easily
  4. When it reaches the right point, pour it on a jar and just cool it off for a few minutes before trying it out yourself! Voil√°, strawberry jelly!
